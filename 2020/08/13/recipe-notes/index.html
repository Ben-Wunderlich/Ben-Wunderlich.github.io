recipe-notes
2020-08-13 #Javascript

My mom was annoyed she couldn’t keep track of modifications she made for online recipes, I thought a good solution could be a browser extension that lets you keep notes on each recipe.

As it turns out, it is not very hard to make a browser extension, and what I came up with lets you attach notes to urls, and has a separate page you can open to see and manage all the notes you have made.

My mom never used it, but I found some surprising uses. Because there is nothing specific to recipes, I kept track of other things, like progress through a book and favorite quotes. Because it also keeps track of the links, I use it almost like a bookmark to track things.

The one thing I neglected was adding a timestamp to each entry, so they can’t be ordered by date, but it is still pretty useful and I regularly use it.

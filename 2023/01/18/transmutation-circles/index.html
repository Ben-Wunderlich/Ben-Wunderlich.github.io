transmutation-circles
2023-01-18 #Art #Javascript

A friend of mine decided to run a game set in the world of fullmetal alchemist(one of my favorite anime), and as I was thinking about having a character in the world, I wondered what kind of transmutation circle they would have. Then I remembered, that is the sort of thing I make! So I looked at some reference, opened a p5js project and went wild with it.

basic hero circles

I started a little more simple, trying to match the style from the show, something a protagonist could use, maybe a minor character that uses ice or light transmutation.

next I wanted an evil one, something to have ominous music played when it appears onscreen, so of course that means lots of eyes, spirals and some perlin noise symbols for chaos.

pushing away

After the first few I wanted to diverge from the shows style more, so I looked through my previous images to find some inspiration starting with the sierpinski triangle(a limited version).

greek transmutation

Another thing I had seen recently was some ancient greek vases, so I made one inspired by them.

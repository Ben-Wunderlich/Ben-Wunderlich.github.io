2023-08-03 #Meta

Close the the end of my computer science degree, I realized I had made a lot of projects and was starting to forget about some of them. So I decided to make a blog to chronicle them.

The first decision to make was which framework to use, I looked through forum posts, top 10 lists and an avalanche of wordpress ads. I wanted something that was made for developers so I could

  • write in markdown and compile to html
  • easily adapt it to any type of post I wanted to make
  • embed other websites I had made in the posts

I settled on hexo because although it was a little less popular than many options, it had everything I wanted and a library of themes to use and adapt to my needs.

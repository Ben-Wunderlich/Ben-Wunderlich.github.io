reflections
2022-11-25 #Art #Javascript

I saw a sunset reflected in a lake, and knew I had to try and recreate it. After looking at photos I saw the distortions offset the appearence of the reflection horizontally and increased the closer to the viewer. I chose to model the distortions offsets as sine waves, whose amplitudes increased closer to the viewer.

In short, I can use a variation on one of my favourite functions, x*sin(x) as the distortion function.
